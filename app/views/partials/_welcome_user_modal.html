Welcome to MarketRex!

Your arrival is a very big deal to us. To get the most out of your MarketRex experience, we encourge you to follow these 4 simple steps in chronological order

  1. 1. Complete your profile
  2. 2. Next, add some data
  3. 3. Next, you'll be ready to connect
  4. 4. Once connected, you can begin your search

P.S. Don't forget, by forming connections, your data and your connection's data become instantly searchable by each other.

P.S.S. But don't worry, until you approve an unlock request, your data is locked at all times (in search results) when viewed by any of your connections.
